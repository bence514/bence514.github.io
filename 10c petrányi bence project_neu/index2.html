Introduction Equipment Education Eco-school
Our school, which is specialized in computer technology, has been named after John von Neumann. While learning here, the students will learn the basic knowledge and receive a high level of I.T. knowledge. We are able to train disabled students, students with special needs (i.e. students with sight- or hearing problems, physical disabilities as well as students with learning difficulties).
We are equipped with 30 classrooms, 15 computer labs and a gym. The classroms are equipped with the required things you need for learning (based on my classroom the 233).The labs are equipped with computers of the latest technology, with Internet and intranet, they are also maintained often. There are some slow computers but all of them is more than good enough for working.
The teachers are doing their best to prepare the students for their graduaation in 12th grade. The english classes will make you more than equipped for the language exam on B2 level or C1 at best. There is an opportunity for students to do an early graduation which the teachers will prepare them for. On some lessons the classes will be divided into groups for better education.

Eco school

Environmental protection takes up a big role in our school. We got the name of eco-school in the name of Ministry of National Resources and the Ministry of Rural Development from the Institute for Educational Research and Development and we still have it today. It opens up a lot of opportunities for the students like events and visiting locations. You can tell this from looking at the school too. Just look at the furniture made by the students and the teachers. They are made out of used tyres and computer cases. We participate in many events centered on environmental protection such as BISEL where we measured the nearby rivers wildlife and contamination level.
